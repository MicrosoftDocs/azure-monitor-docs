# Ops360 FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Ops360.

## General
**Q:** What is Ops360?
**A:** Ops360 is a unified operations management platform for cloud and hybrid environments.

## Onboarding
**Q:** How do I onboard my team?
**A:** Use the onboarding guide and invite users via the portal.

## Licensing
**Q:** What licensing options are available?
**A:** Per-user and consumption-based models are supported.

## Security
**Q:** How is my data protected?
**A:** Ops360 uses Azure AD authentication and encrypts data at rest and in transit.

## Support
**Q:** Where can I get help?
**A:** Access support through the portal or contact your Microsoft representative.
