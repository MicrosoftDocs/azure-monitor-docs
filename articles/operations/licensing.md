# Ops360 Licensing

Ops360 offers flexible licensing options to meet the needs of organizations of all sizes. Understanding licensing is key to maximizing value and ensuring compliance.

## Licensing Models
- **Per-user licensing:** Ideal for organizations with predictable user counts.
- **Consumption-based licensing:** Scales with usage, suitable for dynamic environments.

## License Management
- Assign licenses via the Ops360 portal or Azure Marketplace
- Monitor license usage and renewals in the dashboard
- Automated alerts for approaching limits

## Compliance
Ops360 is designed to meet enterprise compliance standards. Review your organization's requirements and configure settings accordingly.

## Support
For licensing questions, contact your Microsoft account representative or use the portal's support features.
