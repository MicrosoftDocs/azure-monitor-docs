# Agentic Experience in Ops360

Ops360 introduces an agentic experience, leveraging AI-driven guidance and automation to enhance operational workflows.

## What is Agentic Experience?
Agentic experience refers to interactive, context-aware assistance that helps users troubleshoot, automate, and optimize operations.

## Key Capabilities
- Guided troubleshooting with step-by-step recommendations
- Automated remediation for common issues
- Contextual insights based on telemetry and logs
- Integration with Azure Copilot and other AI services

## Benefits
- Reduces mean time to resolution (MTTR)
- Empowers users with actionable insights
- Improves operational efficiency

## Getting Started
Explore agentic features in the Ops360 portal under the "Agentic Experience" section.
